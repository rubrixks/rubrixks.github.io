The Game.

What is "The Game"?

This is one of the oldest jokes on the internet, created long ago to troll anyone who has ever heard of this game.
The concept is simple. This is a mental game where the objective is to not think about The Game. Ever. That's it.
I'd imagine most people go throughout their lives without ever hearing of The Game. Those are the lucky ones.
There are variations to the rules of The Game, but for this iteration here are the rules:
1.A player loses the game the moment the concept of The Game registers onto their minds.
2.The player then has to announce to the internet they lost The Game.
3.Thereafter, don't think about it again. The Game never ends... it ends for the player when they croak.

Why use Ethereum to announce you lost The Game?

You can announce your loss in many different ways. You can announce it to those around you. You can post it on your social media.
But now you can post it on the Ethereum blockchain.
This method is better for maintaining a global scoreboard. Through other sources it is difficult to measure metrics of The Game.
With this method of recording, anyone can follow who has lost The Game, how often it thought about, and how The Game spreads through a network. All with a sort of anonymity.
As a community we can learn much about ourselves by having this information on a decentralized blockchain.
